Photography exhibited at Coastal Arts League

By Sid Gandhi
March 11, 2017

My digital photography was exhibited along with other artists from technology companies at the Coastal Arts League gallery in Half Moon Bay, California.

“I try and understand as much detail as possible about the subject so I can shoot it optimally,” said Gandhi. “Even so, when I look at the photograph I made, there are always interesting details I didn’t perceive in the moment.”
