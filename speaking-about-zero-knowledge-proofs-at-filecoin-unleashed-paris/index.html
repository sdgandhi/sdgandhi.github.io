Speaking about zero knowledge proofs at Filecoin Unleashed Paris

By Sid Gandhi
July 18, 2023

Filecoin Unleashed was an event put together by Protocol Labs (the creators of Filecoin) during EthCC 2023 in Paris.

I was invited to speak about how Polybase is helping a Filecoin team manage their database of service provider reputation in a decentralized and secure way on Polybase.

I also touch on some of the innovations in ZK (zero knowledge cryptography) that Polybase is working on.
