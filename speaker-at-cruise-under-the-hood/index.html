Speaker at Cruise Under the Hood

By Sid Gandhi
March 21, 2022

Under the Hood was a special event held by Cruise to deep dive into technical topics in the autonomous vehicle space. I was the speaker for the section titled "Accelerating AV Development", which covered the newest AV simulation technologies Cruise is building and why they're important to the long term viability of Cruise's business. I structured my talk as a narrative, detailing the various technologies the Simulation team has built and how they come together in a thoughtful way to solve some long-standing problems in AV simulation.

Multiple news sites picked up my quotes from the talk including TechCrunch and MIT Technology Review.
