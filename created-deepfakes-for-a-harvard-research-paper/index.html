Created deepfakes for a Harvard research paper

By Sid Gandhi
January 13, 2021

DFBlue announced a partnership with Harvard University in Nov 2019 for a research study on the psychological effects of political deepfakes ahead of the upcoming 2020 U.S. elections.

Title says it all: "Political Deepfake Videos Misinform the Public, But No More than Other Fake Media"
