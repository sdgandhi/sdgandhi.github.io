Launched Ketchup to help people stay in touch with friends and family during COVID-19

By Sid Gandhi
August 20, 2020

The Ketchup app launch appeared on the front page of Product Hunt.

We find ourselves moving from one task to the next, one notification after another. We don't get the idle time of old when our thoughts might drift to a friend or family member.

If we could keep in touch with our loved ones we would have more fulfilling relationships and feel less lonely. Ketchup helps you do just that, keep in touch. Not with profiles, but with meaningful conversations...like the old days.
