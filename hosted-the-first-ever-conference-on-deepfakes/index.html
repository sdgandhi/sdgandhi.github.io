Hosted the first-ever conference on Deepfakes 

By Sid Gandhi
October 25, 2019

On Sept. 5, DFBlue hosted an event in New York City called “Deepfake — Fake News or Powerful tool?” which was a discussion on what’s possible with Deepfake technology and how it can be used ethically.

The questions we answered are:

  • What is possible with today’s deepfake technology?
  • How can we detect deepfakes?
  • What are the ethical uses of deepfakes in the world of Advertising & Entertainment?
